The Inaugural Fitz Enterprise Annual Dinner

on May 3, 2016

It is our pleasure to invite you to The Inaugural Fitz Enterprise Annual Dinner on Saturday 5th March. The dinner is for both current students and alumni and will include a short mentoring session before the dinner where alumni can share advice and experiences with budding young entrepreneurs.

The mentoring session will start at 6pm in The Old SCR. If you are interested in entrepreneurship, have a business idea of your own or are just curious about your career path after Fitz; this dinner is the place to be. We have a broad range of alumni attending the event, ranging from veteran entrepreneurs to young start-up founders to management consultants. Our intimate mentoring session will provide you with the opportunity to tap into the wealth of experiences and advice that our alumni have to offer. If you are interested in attending the event, we ask that you tell us your specific area of interest, so that we can match you with a relevant alumnus for the mentoring session.
